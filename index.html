Welcome To Government Polytechnic Jaunpur

Government Polytechnic Jaunpur, founded in 1984, is a government institute of technical education in Jagdishpur, Jaunpur, Uttar Pradesh, India. It is affiliated with the Uttar Pradesh Board of Technical Education (BTEUP) and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).
